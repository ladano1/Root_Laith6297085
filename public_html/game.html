How to Play!

Each player must pick one player piece to use for the rest of the game. This will represent where you are on the board.

Choose a player to start, the turns will continue in a clockwise rotation. The first player will roll the dice and advance to the square that corresponds to your roll. You will either land on a Questions or Action square. You must read aloud and follow the instructions from ced card.

If you are able to complete the task, you may keep your player piece on that square. If you fail to do so, you must remain where your Player piece was at the start of your turn.

To earn points, you must answer the most questions. Each question card has a different levels.The darker the yellow, the harder the questions. These will be worth the most points in reward tokens. Hopefully this will encourage you to share and listen to others in order to win the game. As the game goes on you will continue to collect your reward tokens.

The player to complete the hike with the most reward tokens wins!
